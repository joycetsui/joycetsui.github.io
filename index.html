About

I am a fourth year computer science and business administration student studying both at the University of Waterloo and Wilfred Laurier University. I hope to complete my degree with a concentration in Software Engineering and an option in accounting. I grew up with a passion for technology after watching my dad, who also works in the technology industry, take apart computers all day. I always found it amazing that machines could do so much with such little effort. My goal one day is to use these powerful machines to create social change within the world in which I live. In my free time, I try to get as involved within the community as possible. I enjoy volunteering with a variety of clubs and services within the universities and the community. I am also a passionate dancer, having danced for most of life, and try to continue some sort of training in university. Check out my resume for more information about my technical and volunteer experience or contact me for further information.
